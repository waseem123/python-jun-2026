Operators - are the symbols to perform any kind of operation on data 
Operands - the values or variable on which the operation is being performed

Types of Operators in Python
    1. Arithmetic Operators
        +, -, *, /, //, %, **
    2. Assignment Operators
        =, +=, -=, *=, /=, //=, %=, **=
    3. Relational | Comparison | Conditional Operators
        <, >, <=, >=, ==, !=
    4. Logical Operators
        and, or, not
    5. Bitwise Operators
        &, |
    6. Shift Operators
        <<, >>
    7. Identity Operators
        is, is not
    8. Membership Operators
        in, not in
